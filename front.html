About Us

Welcome to Expense Tracker, your personal finance management tool designed to help you take control of your expenses and savings. Our mission is to provide a simple, intuitive platform that empowers you to make informed financial decisions.

Key Features

  • Expense Tracking: Easily log and categorize your daily expenses to see where your money goes.
  • Budgeting Tools: Set monthly budgets and get alerts when you're nearing your limits.
  • Financial Reports: Generate detailed reports and visualizations to analyze your spending habits.
  • Multi-Device Sync: Access your data from anywhere with seamless synchronization across devices.
  • Secure Data: Your financial information is protected with top-tier security measures.

Our Technology Stack

Expense Tracker is built using modern web technologies to ensure a smooth and responsive user experience:

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3,
  • Backend: JavaScript (React.js)
